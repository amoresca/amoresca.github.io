My Story

I received a BA in Psychology from Simmons University. Despite enjoying the subject, I realized upon graduation that I didn't want to become a psychologist. So, I spent some time teaching English while living in South Korea. In my free time there, I learned how to code and discovered that I really liked it. After coming back to America, I pursued a career in web development full time.

Experience

As a frontend web developer at EffectiV Internet Solutions, I built custom websites integrated with WordPress as well as HTML emails and other marketing materials. I helped manage hosting and website updates for 15-20 clients and was in charge of maintaining the health and security of our servers. On a daily basis, I worked directly with clients to implement feature requests and fix bugs.

Capstone Project

Borrowr

Personally built a web app that facilitates the process of borrowing things from friends and keeping track of where your stuff is. Users can sign up for an account, create lists of items that they’re willing to lend, connect with their friends on the platform, and request to borrow items. The backend is Ruby on Rails with a PostgreSQL database, and the frontend is built with Vue.js and Bootstrap.
